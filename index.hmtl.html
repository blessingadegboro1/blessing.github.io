FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about working with me.

How long does a typical project take?

Timelines vary by project scope. A simple website takes 5–10 days; a full e-commerce store with SEO and listings typically takes 2–4 weeks. I'll give you a clear timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with clients outside Nigeria?

Absolutely! I work with clients globally via Zoom, Google Meet, WhatsApp, and email. I've successfully served clients in the UK, USA, Canada, and across Africa.

What information do you need to get started?

For most projects I need your business goals, target audience, brand assets (logo, colours if available), product/service details, and any design references you like. I'll guide you through everything in our kick-off call.

How do payments work?

I typically work with a 50% deposit upfront and 50% on completion. For longer projects, a milestone-based payment structure is available. I accept bank transfer, PayPal, and other methods.

Do you offer ongoing support after the project?

Yes! I offer monthly retainer packages for ongoing SEO, social media management, email marketing, and website maintenance. Many clients continue working with me long after their initial project.
